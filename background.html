Background

I have been working as a finance contractor for a number of years in different sectors.

I think it is best if I start with my most recent experience first and then work backwards.

Closerstill Media Limited - Events company

I worked at Closerstill Media for 7 months. The company was transferring their finance system from Sage to Unit 4. I had previously worked with some of the employees at Wolseley.

My first task was to reconcile the data from Sage to Unit4 ensuring that the overall numbers tied back.

Secondly, I was required to learn Prevero and import data into this system ready for it to be used by the business for reporting purposes.

Halfords - Retail

I worked at Halfords for 10 months. I worked in their Operations Finance team. This team was responsible for reportng on the performance of stores.

I designed and implemented new year-to-date reports that looked at trends. This enabled Operations to take a long-term view of performance.

I also provided support to Operations with adhoc reports and in-depth analysis based on their requirements.

Wolseley - Retail

I worked at Wolseley for nearly 2 years. I worked in various roles in their Finance team.

I was brought into the project team to develop Business Objects and Hyperion reports. These were part of the new company wide reporting suite.

I then moved to develop a new commissions process together with new models and documentation.

National Grid - Utilities

I joined at a time when the distribution part of the business was being seperated

My role was to document the current working processes and the systems that were being used
